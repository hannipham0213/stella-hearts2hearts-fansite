News & Timeline

Stella's journey from trainee to rising K-pop star

2007.06.18

Born in Ulsan, South Korea

Kim Da-hyun was born in Ulsan, South Korea. Moved to Vancouver, Canada at age 4-5 and spent 12 years there.

Early 2023

Joined SM Entertainment

After being cast by 32 out of 60 agencies at Music Doctor Academy's in-house audition in Busan, Stella chose SM Entertainment. Her audition song was "Like A Dream" by Chrisette Michele.

2024

Viral Pre-debut Dance Video

A video of Stella dancing to LE SSERAFIM's "Antifragile" went viral, gaining ~498k views and generating buzz before her official debut.

2025.01

SM Announces Hearts2Hearts

SM Entertainment officially revealed their new 8-member girl group. Stella provided the English narration for the group's debut trailer.

2025.02.24

Official Debut — "The Chase"

Hearts2Hearts debuted with single album "The Chase", featuring title track "The Chase" and B-side "Butterflies". The album sold 408,800 copies in its first week, setting a new record for K-pop girl group debuts.

2025.03.11

First Music Show Win 🏆

Only 15 days after debut, Hearts2Hearts won their first music show trophy on "The Show" — the fastest first win in SM Entertainment history.

2025.06.18

18th Birthday + Digital Single "STYLE"

Stella celebrated her 18th birthday on June 18, 2025. Hearts2Hearts also released the digital single "STYLE" on this day.

2025.09

Pokemon Collaboration — "Pretty Please"

Hearts2Hearts released "Pretty Please" as a pre-release track in collaboration with Pokemon, featuring cute, playful choreography.

2025.10

First Mini Album — "FOCUS"

Hearts2Hearts released their first mini album "FOCUS" with a mature new concept. Stella's favorite B-side is "Apple Pie".

2025 (Year End)

9 Rookie Awards

In their debut year, Hearts2Hearts won 9 rookie awards including Seoul Music Awards "Rookie of the Year" and K-World Dream Awards "Super Rookie".
