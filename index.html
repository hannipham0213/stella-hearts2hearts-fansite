About Stella

Stella (Kim Dahyun) is a Korean-Canadian singer and member of Hearts2Hearts, SM Entertainment's newest 8-member girl group. Born in Ulsan, South Korea and raised in Vancouver, Canada, she brings a unique international charm to the K-pop scene. Known for her bright eye smile, cat-like personality, and multi-instrumental talents, Stella has quickly become a fan favorite since her debut in February 2025.
